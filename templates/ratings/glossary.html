{% translate "Rating Tiers" %}

{# --- Halal --- #}
check_circle {% translate "Halal" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Permissible. Games assessed to have minimal concerns. Any potentially problematic elements are minor, incidental, easily avoidable, and not positively normalized. Core themes and required actions are permissible.{% endblocktranslate %}
{# --- Mashbouh --- #}
warning {% translate "Mashbouh" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Doubtful / Caution Advised. Games contain elements of moderate concern ('M' severity) that may be difficult to avoid or are somewhat normalized, OR significant scholarly differences exist regarding key elements. Player vigilance or adjustments are often required to mitigate risks (e.g., Fitnah, normalization). Falls short of the Haram threshold.{% endblocktranslate %}
{# --- Haram --- #}
cancel {% translate "Haram" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Impermissible. Games feature clear and significant problematic content. This includes: unavoidable simulation or required engagement in major sins ('P' severity), high-level Aqidah concerns ('H' severity), multiple severe issues ('H' severity in key categories), or pervasive positive normalization/promotion of Haram making the game's environment contrary to Islamic values.{% endblocktranslate %}
{# --- Kufr / Shirk --- #}
gpp_bad {% translate "Kufr" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Disbelief / Polytheism. Games containing required participation in, or unavoidable positive promotion/normalization of, acts of Shirk (associating partners with Allah), Kufr (disbelief), ritualistic magic (Sihr), blasphemy, or direct attacks on core Islamic tenets ('P' severity in Aqidah).{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "Severity Levels (for Breakdown Categories)" %}

{% translate "None (N)" %}
{% blocktranslate %}No significant concerns identified regarding direct action or normalization.{% endblocktranslate %}
{% translate "Low (L)" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Minor issues present, potentially avoidable, infrequent, or contextually less impactful. Not presented positively or normalized. Minimal risk.{% endblocktranslate %}
{% translate "Medium (M)" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Moderate concerns. Problematic elements (not major sins/Kufr) may be present and optional OR somewhat normalized neutrally/slightly positively. Requires player vigilance.{% endblocktranslate %}
{% translate "High (H)" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Severe concerns. Problematic actions (short of major sins/Kufr) strongly encouraged/rewarded OR Haram elements difficult to avoid OR impermissible lifestyles/beliefs (short of Kufr/Shirk) actively promoted/normalized. High risk of negative influence/Tashabbuh.{% endblocktranslate %}
{% translate "Prohibited (P)" %}
{% blocktranslate %}Explicitly involves required/unavoidable simulation/engagement in major sins or acts of Kufr/Shirk, OR central, inescapable, positive promotion/normalization of such.{% endblocktranslate %}
