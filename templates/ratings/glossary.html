{# --- Mashbouh --- #}
warning {% translate "Mashbouh" %}
{% translate "Doubtful / Caution Advised. Engagement requires careful consideration and Taqwa (God-consciousness). Avoid if in doubt." %}
{# --- Haram --- #}
cancel {% translate "Haram" %}
{% translate "Impermissible. Games featuring clear and unavoidable prohibited content or core mechanics integral to the gameplay that involve committing or simulating major sins, promoting vice, or containing themes fundamentally opposed to Islamic morality." %}
{# --- Kufr / Shirk --- #}
gpp_bad {% translate "Kufr" %}
{% translate "Disbelief / Polytheism. Games containing explicit promotion, normalization, or requirement of participation in acts of Shirk (associating partners with Allah) or Kufr (disbelief), blasphemy, veneration of false deities, practice of magic (Sihr), or direct attacks on core Islamic tenets." %}
